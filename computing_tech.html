Hibernation Pods:

The spaceship in the movie, the Starship Avalon, uses advanced hibernation pods that allow passengers to sleep for extended periods during the long journey through space. These pods are equipped with advanced life support and monitoring systems to ensure the safety of the passengers while in hibernation.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) System:

The Avalon is controlled by an advanced AI system. This system manages various ship functions, provides information to passengers, and maintains the ship's systems. In Addition, Arthur, the bartender in the movie, is portrayed as a sophisticated AI that can interact with passengers in a human-like manner.

Entertainment Systems:

Passengers on the Avalon have access to advanced entertainment systems, including virtual reality experiences and interactive media. These systems provide entertainment and relaxation during the long voyage.

Autodoc:

The passangers on the Avalon have futuristic technologies in terms of healthcare. This Autodoc is able to diagnose and treat essentially every disease that can be, and as see in the movie, Jim is successfully resussitated by the Autodoc.

Spacesuit:

The spacesuits provided furutistic abilities such as having magnetic boots that atatch to the spaceship, as well as tethers that magnetically click on at various points throughout the spaceship.

Communication Systems:

The Avalon has communication systems that enable passengers to stay connected with Earth and other parts of the galaxy. These systems allow for real-time communication and data exchange.
